Here's what you can do about it...

Below are links to very easy-to-use privacy tools and opt-out pages of major digital advertising groups and social networks.

Companies won't respect your personal beliefs unless you make them known. Make your browser work for you by taking advantage of these tools and opt-outs!

Privacy Tools

Ghostery, uBlock, HTTPS Everywhere, and Lightbeam are very useful tools that prevent unwanted tracking from advertising companies and keep your online activity safe. You can also enable the "Do Not Track" header in your browser and disable third party cookies.

Advertiser Opt-Outs

Visit the Digital Advertising Alliance's Consumer Choice Page and Ghostery Enterprise and follow the instructions to opt-out of tracking for interest-based ads by all companies participating in each page.

Social Network Preferences

Facebook and Twitter have internal opt-outs that keep those networks from tracking your data as well.

Finally...

Share this website with your other friends on Facebook! They should know exactly how Facebook is using their personal information and what they can do to protect themselves. Help make protected browsing the new normal!
